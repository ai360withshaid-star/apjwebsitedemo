01 — ABOUT

From load tests to language models.

A decade and a half of shipping systems that have to actually work, now applied to AI products that businesses bet on.

I started out in performance testing and engineering, working with global enterprises across Europe to keep critical systems fast and reliable. JMeter, LoadRunner, NeoLoad, and the kind of production fires that teach you respect for real-world systems.

Today I design and ship generative AI products, voice agents, and intelligent automation for businesses across India and the Gulf. I run S.H.A.A.I Solutions for client work and create content under AI360 with Shaid to teach what I learn along the way.

The thread connecting both chapters: building things that hold up under load, whether that load is 10,000 concurrent users or a customer asking your AI agent something weird at 2am.
